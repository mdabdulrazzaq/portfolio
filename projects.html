Projects

Emotion AI: Facial Key-Points Detection

Developed a deep learning model using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Residual Networks (ResNets) to enhance feature extraction and prediction accuracy for facial key-point detection. This enabled real-time emotion recognition with high precision in detecting key facial landmarks, paving the way for applications in security and healthcare.

BCI-SSVEP Detection

Designed a multi-task learning deep learning model to process EEG signals for Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) applications. This model aids in the early detection of glaucoma by mapping visual field responses more efficiently than traditional methods. It also offers a more objective and quicker diagnostic procedure.
